The word "greatness" simply means the "quality" or state of being "great" Historically, mahatma gandhi, has and will always be a great man, mahatma was a man full of gretaness untill his last breathe, he was affirmatly a man with large interest in the wellbeing of his country, Mohandas karamchand Gandhi has he was first known , was born on the 2nd october 1869 into a gujarati hindu modh bania family in porbandar (also known as sudamapuri) a costal town on the kathiawar peninsula and part of the small princely state of porbandar in the kathiawar Agency of the British Raj. His father, karamchand uttamchand Gandhi(who was born on 1822 and died 1885), served as the dewan (chief minister) of porbandar state. his family, originated from the then village of kutiana in what was then junagadh state.
